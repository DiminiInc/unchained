Settings

Blocked sites

Block all access to website.
One domain (e.g. "example.com") per line.

Blocked words

Block pages containing specific words.
One word per line.

Partially blocked sites

Block access to specific website's sections.
One section (e.g. "example.com/games") per line.

Partially allowed sites

Block access to website, except specific section.
One entry per line. Entry should consist of domain and allowed section divided by space (e.g. "example.com /messages")
